Our Social Enterprise Mission

PT. Dian Niaga is a social enterprise. Our commitment to quality is matched by our dedication to social impact and community empowerment.

Local Community Empowerment

Approximately 30% of our raw material processing is managed by local communities. We provide comprehensive training, necessary equipment, and full logistical support (including transport) to ensure consistent quality and, most importantly, a reliable and fair income for participants.

Inclusivity & Support

Our dedicated training center actively caters to and supports individuals with disabilities. We offer specialized training and resources to integrate them directly into our supply chain, fostering economic independence, social inclusion, and valuable skill development.
